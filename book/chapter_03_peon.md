# Chapter Three: The Peon's Account

---

Boss say go hill. Me go.

Hill steep. Me not like hill. Hill make legs hurt. Legs already hurt from yesterday. And day before. Always legs hurt. That just life for peon.

Other one come too. Skinny one. Human. Geralt. He not talk much. Me not mind. Me not talk much either. Talk take energy. Energy for dig.

Me have hammer. Me have wood. Wood heavy. Why wood always heavy? Tree standing up, look normal. Chop tree down, now whole thing heavy. That not fair.

We dig foundation first. Dig dig dig. Me good at dig. Me been digging since... long time. Very long time. Maybe whole life. Hard say. Days all look same when you peon.

Fighting happen nearby. Loud. Crash crash, scream, more crash. Me try not listen. Listening make you think. Thinking make work slow. Work slow, boss angry. Boss angry, peon get yelled at. Yelling no fun.

Geralt look over at fighting. He look worried. Me tell him: "You not look. You just work." He nod. Smart human.

We work. Sun move across sky. Sky not care about us. That fine. Me not care about sky either. We even.

Noon come. Geralt give me half a roll. HALF A ROLL! Me so happy me almost cry. Me not cry though. Peon not cry. ...Me cry little bit. Just small cry. Nobody see.

More work. Wall go up. Me hit thumb with hammer. Me say word boss not like. Nobody there hear. Except Geralt. He look other way. Good human.

Then arrow.

Arrow hit Geralt. Geralt sit down. Geralt look at arrow like: "where this come from?" Me know where it come from. Battlefield. Battlefield full of arrows. People act surprised every time.

Me go to Geralt. Me not know lots of things. Me not know why sky blue. Me not know why hill so steep. Me not know why boss always want tower RIGHT NOW, IMMEDIATELY, STOP BEING SLOW. But me know this: press cloth on hole. That stop bleeding. Peon learn that early.

Me press cloth on Geralt. Geralt say "ow." Me say "yes."

That all we say. But that enough.

Later they come get Geralt. He alive. Good. Me glad.

Tower get finished. Me finish it. Alone. Take longer alone. Everything take longer alone. That just life too.

Captain look at tower. Captain nod. Captain say nothing to me. That fine. Me used to it.

Me look at finished tower. Big tower. Good tower.

...Me build that.

Me not always just dig.
