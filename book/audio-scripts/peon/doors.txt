Morgum think about this question many times. Door question is harder than it look. Morgum will say what is good about door, and what is bad about door, and then Morgum will decide. This is how Morgum solve problems. Sometimes.

Good things about door: wind not come in. Rain not come in. Animals not come in — mostly. If animal is clever enough to open the door, then door is not doing very much, but most animals not that clever. Dog cannot open door. Wolf cannot open door. Murloc usually cannot open door, although Morgum have seen a murloc work a door handle for a very long time in a way that was uncomfortable to watch. So: door keep out most things. Also, door tell you where the inside is. This is useful when it is dark or you are tired or you have been away and forgotten which wall has the hole in it for going through. You walk toward door and then you are in the inside. This is a genuine service the door provide.

But here is the problem Morgum have with door. Door must be opened. Every single time. You come home with arms full of rocks — you always have rocks, there are always rocks to be moved — and the door is just there. Standing there. Waiting. Both hands have rocks. Now what? You put down the rocks. You open the door. You pick up the rocks. Then you go in. This happen every day, for your whole life. Morgum have not done the counting exactly but it is a very large number of times to put down rocks. The door asks this of you and does not apologize.

Spikes around the entrance: you do not open spikes. Things that should not come in, they do not come in. Same result as door. But also: things that should come in, cannot come in because of spikes. So is a problem. Similar to locked door but worse because you also hurt yourself trying to solve it. Morgum know this from experience. Morgum have counted. But maybe: door for normal times, spikes for when you wish to be alone and are very serious about it. This is compromise. Morgum is good at compromise when the first thing does not work. Morgum's answer to the door question is: probably door. Maybe door. Door is likely correct. Morgum will continue to think.
