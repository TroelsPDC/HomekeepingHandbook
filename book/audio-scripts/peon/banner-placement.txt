Banner useful. Morgum like banner. Banner do two things: one, tell everyone where Morgum is; two, can hit thing with banner if thing need hitting. Both important. Together: very important. Peasant say banner is "statement." Morgum agree. Banner say: Peon here. This Peon place. That is full statement.

Morgum have five banner. One old one, from first big work. Is torn now but Morgum keep it. Have feeling about old banner. Second banner is red — Morgum work better with red banner, run faster, carry more, hit harder. Red banner for heavy days. Third banner has gold edge. Morgum not know where gold edge banner come from. Found it near road after big battle. Probably very important banner. Keeping it for important day. Will know important day when it arrive. Four and five are same banner but Morgum make them: both say "Peon" because that what Morgum is and that what this territory is. Very clear. Other peons understand immediately.

Banner don't need neat. Peasant say neat banner. But neat banner is banner that not been working. Torn banner, muddy banner, banner wrapped around axe handle because handle break and banner was closest thing — this is working banner. This is banner that understand what banner is for. Territory don't care if banner neat. Territory just need to know it is territory. A banner that has been many places has earned the looking of it.

Rotation important. Morgum rotate banner by season and by job. Red banner for summer and lifting. Dark banner for tunneling. Gold edge banner Morgum saving. When exactly right important day come, Morgum will know immediately. The gold edge banner is ready. Has been ready long time. Very patient banner.
