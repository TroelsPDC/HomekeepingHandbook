Fungus is best thing. Morgum will explain.

You go to sleep, fungus comes. You go to work, fungus is still there when you come back. You forget about fungus for three weeks, fungus has made more fungus. This is productivity. This is worker with initiative. Morgum respects this. Morgum wishes to manage more workers who are like fungus. Very reliable. Never complain. Never ask for day off.

Morgum has three patches. First patch: under the loose board by the water barrel, been there since the second year when the roof leaked. Good patch. Reliable. Small brown mushrooms, smell like earth and something from deeper than earth. Morgum has eaten many. Has not died yet. This is Morgum's main review of them: still alive. Five stars. Second patch: under the big board in the east corner. This one grows in the shape of a circle sometimes. Morgum does not go inside the circle. But the mushrooms on the outside of the circle are fine, Morgum thinks. Morgum eats the outside ones. Is probably fine.

Third patch glows. Started glowing in the autumn of last year, maybe year before, Morgum loses track. It is a green-white colour, like sick moonlight, and it pulses slow, like breathing but not like any breathing Morgum wants to stand next to for too long. But Morgum is very proud. Morgum has shown everyone. Most people back away fast, which is their choice. But Morgum uses it to find the water bucket in the night now. Does not need a torch. You just walk toward the east corner and the glow shows you where everything is, and also the glow shows you there is a spider in the corner that was always there but now you can see it, which is information Morgum is still deciding how to feel about. Overall: glowing mushroom better than not glowing mushroom. Morgum recommends.
